What we really do?

We sell, service, deliver, rent, and ship all of our equipment both locally and nationally. We offer a 12,000 square foot showroom with equipment on display for our local buyers. We have a fully dedicated Parts and Service Department as well as a group of technicians who can service/repair both in-house and at your location. 

Our Offerings

Our services not only include sales of new and used equipment but we also have additional offerings. Our Parts and Service Department has stock of over 6,000 parts on hand. We provide a short term and long term equipment rental program for businesses and homes. We also rent out our showroom for birthday parties, holidays, and events.

History

Family owned and operated, Game Exchange of Colorado's 75,000 s.f. facility makes us the Rocky Mountain region's largest supplier of home entertainment games & equipment. For more than 30 years, we have been one of the country's most experienced suppliers for all of your home and commercial entertainment needs. Our experience in the industry allows us to facilitate your needs and identify solutions quickly and efficiently.
